OUR SERVICES

Braiding

Our skilled braiders will meticulously section your hair and create small, uniform braids that can be styled in various ways but also provide protection for your natural hair and allow for low-maintenance styling options. Customized with various patterns and designs for a unique look.

Nails

Express your creativity and make a statement with our nail art services. Our talented nail technicians can create intricate designs, patterns, and embellishments on your nails using a variety of techniques, including hand-painted designs, decals, glitter, and more. From simple accents to elaborate masterpieces, the options are endless.

Lashes

Enhance the beauty of your natural lashes with classic lash extensions. Our skilled lash technicians will individually apply a single extension to each of your natural lashes, giving you a longer, fuller, and more defined lash line, If you desire a more dramatic and glamorous look our technicians will apply lash extensions ideal for those who desire extra fullness and density.

Hairstyling

Our skilled hairstylists are trained to create trendy and flattering hairstlyes for women. Whether you're looking for a classic bob, a trendy pixie cut, or a stylish layered look, we'll provide a personalized consultation to ensure you leave with a style that suits your style and enhances your features. We'll also finish off your new look with professional styling, using high-quality products to create the perfect finish.

Natural Hair

Give your natural hair the care it deserves with our nourishing and revitalizing hair treatments and hair care , whether your hair needs repair, or a boost of shine, we offer a variety of treatments tailored to your specific needs. we'll help restore and maintain the health and vitality of your hair

Dreading

We understand that dreading styling requires regular maintenance to keep the hair healthy and prevent breakage or damage. Our methods are regular washing, conditioning, and moisturizing, as well as retwisting or tightening the locks as needed. Our Professional maintenance and styling services are available from licensed stylists .
